Nasi Goreng

Description:

Nasi Goreng is a traditional Indonesian fried rice dish that is flavorful and easy to make. It typically includes rice, vegetables, meat or seafood, and is seasoned with a variety of spices and sauces.

Ingredients:

  • A couple of cups of cold cooked rice (day-old works best!)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 boneless chicken breast, chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • A big handful of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons of sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 tablespoon of regular soy sauce
  • A splash of sriracha (adjust to your heat preference)
  • A small handful of chopped green onions for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering (about 350°F).
  2. Crack the eggs into the hot oil and scramble them for about 1 minute until just set, then remove and set aside—this prevents overcooking.
  3. Add the remaining tablespoon of oil to the same wok and sauté the diced onion for 2-3 minutes until translucent.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
  5. Toss in the chopped chicken and cook for 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until no longer pink.
  6. Add the shrimp and cook for 2 minutes until they turn pink and opaque.
  7. Tip: Use cold, day-old rice—it fries better without getting mushy!
  8. Crumble the cold rice into the wok, breaking up any clumps with your spatula.
  9. Pour in the sweet soy sauce, regular soy sauce, and sriracha, then stir-fry everything together for 3-4 minutes until well combined and heated through.
  10. Tip: Don't skimp on the stirring—this ensures every grain gets coated in that delicious sauce.
  11. Fold the scrambled eggs back into the rice mixture and cook for another minute.
  12. Tip: Taste and adjust seasoning now; if you want more heat, add another splash of sriracha.
  13. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions.
  14. Serve immediately with lime wedges on the side.
